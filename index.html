ABOUT THE BREWERY

Black Dog Brewing Co. was founded in 2017 by some questionable characters with a passion for rock music and brewing hand-crafted beers. We brew beers in the name of some of the greatest musicians that walked this earth, and while the music may have stopped, their legacy lives on.

In addition to our selection of finely hand-crafted beers and good music, we serve fresh made-to-order bar snacks and nibbles, as well as a selection of Okanagan wines, B.C. Pops and other good stuff.

"Knowledge speaks, but wisdom listens."

Jimi Hendrix
